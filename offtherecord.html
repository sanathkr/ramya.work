OFF THE RECORD

Fight Traffic Tickets Online

OFF THE RECORD (OTR) - Fight traffic tickets online

Link: offtherecord.com     Stakeholders: Mark Mikhail, Alex Guirguis (Founders)

Off The Record (OTR) is a technology platform that makes it easy for drivers to fight tickets and keep violations off their record. I worked with them to identify major usability problems and provide designs solutions to it. I also gave form to ambitious new ideas such as 3rd party widgets, lawyer discovery platform and helped with their goal to increase users through referrals.

 

MY ROLE: UX Designer

 

TASKS: Usability test, User Research, 

Wireframing, Hi-fi Prototypes,

Interaction design, Widget design

 

First step: Task based cognitive walkthrough

To understand the usability problems of first time users, I tested the website with 8 participants. They were assigned specific tasks starting from discovering the website through Google search, understanding overall service offering, fighting the ticket, all the way through booking a case. This exercise gave me an indication of the major usability issues prevalent in the website.

 

Identify Pain points

Users were excited about the idea of fighting a ticket without showing up to court. Almost all the participants started the process with excitement and continued through the flow. However, as they entered the “Review” step, they were not confident about proceeding to make the payment. Users still had unanswered questions at this point (1) Is this website trustworthy? (2) What happens after I make the payment? (3) How many days will it take to resolve the ticket?

 

Introduce simple visuals and crisp content

Though the current website had all the information clearly laid out in text, it was hard for the user to comprehend how the process worked. I redesigned the “home” page and “how it works” page to have simple words, bullet points, liberal white spaces, a clear call to action and images (speaks a thousand words) to visually explain the entire process. In addition, I also included appropriate keywords, titles, and descriptions that attracted target audience and also gave SEO benefits.

 

Widget design

OTR had partnerships with other high-traffic websites and wanted users to book a case directly from there. I designed a widget for users to begin the process on the third party's website, but eventually land on OTR’s website to complete the transaction.

Inline widget: A small, functional and portable widget that can be embedded into any third party website. Widget continued to have OTR brand colors, allowing users to associate it back to the brand when they eventually land on the website. The widget was designed to have all the necessary tools to take the user through the process in 4 quick steps.

Tools used: Illustrator and Marvelapp

Pop-up widget: A widget design inspired by chat interfaces. The slide up/down interaction was designed to capture the user’s attention at the right place and right time. This widget was designed to take over the full page on mobile screens giving the same experience as a full fledged website.

Tools used: Sketch and Adobe after effects

 

Referral Marketing - Secret Weapon

Off the Record wanted to build a referral program as a part of their marketing strategy. The idea was to consistently engage customers around their “happy experience with the brand” and drive referrals, while also solidifying customer loyalty. After brainstorming, we decided to go in for a double-sided reward program that delivered equal benefits to both sender and receiver. 

 

Platform for Lawyer discovery

Off the Record decided to build a platform for users to reach out to lawyers who specialize in traffic violations. Though there are number of websites like Avvo, Lawyers.com etc. that list lawyers, none of them focused on traffic violations. User’s had a tough time sifting through a generic list. With this research knowledge, I focused on differentiating the service by highlighting OTR’s experience in this area. I also came up with icons for popular traffic violations to visually convey specialized offering.

 

Results

As Off The Record preps for a round of investor funding,  the redesigned website, widget and Lawyer discovery platform is set to increase customer conversions and industry recognition. The website received great reviews from the stakeholders and the latest usability results suggested that users can now navigate the website with more ease.
